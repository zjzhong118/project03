Video Production / The drop Nov/2016

This short animation film has the theme of imagination. It started with a single water drop, then developed to the world of ocean. This film was produced basing on the use of green screen and chroma key. The objects are created by hand drawing to create an organic feeling to the video. The combination of real world and the animation world also gives the audience a feeling of mystery and fantasy.

Process / Before Editing Moodboard

The first step of producing the movie is to create the storyboard to plan out what and where to shot. And the mood board to set the feelings and style of the film.

Storyboard

Creating storyboard to plan out what and where will be for each shot.

handdrawing

Since most part of my film is the animation. So the second step for me to do was to draw the key frames of the animation. Each of them I have to draw at least two, in order to create the effect that pencil keeps drawing on the paper.

Process / Post-production video and sound editing

After transferring all of the images to the computer, I puppet tool on AfterEffects to animate the mermaid, and other sea creatures. For the background, I animated the ocean waves, by using many layers as well, such as the solid layers, colour ramped layer, and the fractal noise layer. And for the fish school, I also used particle tools to make each of the drawings of the fish became alive.

For the sound, I applied both L cut and J cut methods to make the seamless transition for each cut. For the background music and the deep ocean creature sounds, I download them from Archive that has the CC license, And I record the raining, the wind blowing and water dropping sound effects.
