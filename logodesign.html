Logo Design / Umi Jun/2016

The name of the application means “international students’ rice” in Chinese, which indicated the app was as essential as the rice to the international student. Based on the meaning of the application, I started using pencil to sketch my ideas.

Role: Graphic Designer, Concept Developer, Illustrator

Process / First Version

This short animation film has the theme of imagination. It started with a single water drop, then developed to the world of ocean. This film was produced basing on the use of green screen and chroma key. The objects are created by hand drawing to create an organic feeling to the video. The combination of real world and the animation world also gives the audience a feeling of mystery and fantasy.

Process / Final Version

Rather than designing bases on the denotation of the app’s Chinese name, I chose to form the logo in a way that catches the viewers’ eye when they first time see it.

Instead of simply sticking the letter on the character’s belly as a way to show the name of the app, I distorted both of the letters “U” and “M” into the head and body of an alien look character, which adds the technological and advanced characteristics to the application. After refining in the computer, the logo became more memorable due to its simple and unique shape.
