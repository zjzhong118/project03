Logo Design / Umi Jun/2016

The name of the application "UMI" means "International students’ rice" in Chinese; which indicated the app was as essential as the rice to the international student. Based on the meaning of the application, I started using pencil to sketch my ideas.

ROLE: Graphic Designer, Concept Developer, Illustrator

Process / First Version

At the beginning of the logo creation, I followed suggestions from company which was to create a character that has a rice body wearing superman’s cloak, a letter “U” that is placed on the front of the rice man. After refining the logo by Adobe Illustrator, my boss, and other co-workers told me the logo seems has a low quality and cheap design which looks like the logo of street food in night market. As the logo that is showed in figure 1., it looks cute while due to its complex and over-detailed shape, it became less memorable, and cheap looking; as a result I decided to redesign the logo.

Process / Final Version

Rather than designing bases on the denotation of the app’s Chinese name, I chose to form the logo in a way that catches the viewers’ eye when they first time see it.

Instead of simply sticking the letter on the character’s belly as a way to show the name of the app. In figure 2., it shows the transition from the letter U and M to a alien-like logo; I distorted both of the letters “U” and “M” into the head and body of an alien look character, which adds the technological and advanced characteristics to the application. After refining in the computer, the logo became more memorable due to its simple and unique shape. After refining in the computer, the logo became more memorable due to its simple and unique shape.

Final Reflection

During the logo designing process, I have learned that to produce a good design work, only one design is not enough. Moreover, thinking of different design aspects will help generate more inspirations. For instance, a logo can be created based on the connotation of the company or product, but it also can focus on its shape that can catch viewers’ eye.
